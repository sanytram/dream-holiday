FAMOUS JAPANESE CELEBRITIES



Yuzuru Hanyu

A figure skater who has won numerous titles including two Olympic gold medals, known for his artistic performances and technical skills.




Hayao Miyazaki

An iconic film director and animator, co-founder of Studio Ghibli, known for creating some of the most beloved animated films in Japan and around the world.





Hikaru Utada

A highly influential singer-songwriter in Japan, known for her powerful vocals and hit songs that have topped the Japanese music charts.
